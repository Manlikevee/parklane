About #1 Who We Are

At Parklane Montessori Schools, we share a strong value to provide an educational environment that excites and engages students, and instills a lifelong love of learning.

The combination of intimate learning environments, exceptional teaching and broad curriculum enable the unique talents of every child to be recognised and encouraged, and our extra care programmes ensure that our students are happy, confident and emotionally secure.

OUR Features

  • Parklane Montessori Schools currently offers the following levels of education

Cresche

Cresche, where your child's educational journey commences, is a team of professionals committed to honoring your child's emotions and cultivating a sense of responsibility, independence, and freedom of choice..

Nursery

Nursery at Cresche marks the beginning of your child's educational journey, guided by a professional team dedicated to respecting their feelings while instilling a sense of responsibility, independence, and the freedom to make choices.

Primary

Primary School extends our commitment to your child's education, building upon the foundation laid in nursery. Our professional team is dedicated to respecting each child's feelings and promoting responsibility, independence, and the freedom to make choices.
